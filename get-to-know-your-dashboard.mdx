Welcome to Byzantine!

Meet the Dashboard - your central hub to see your balance & returns.

![Screenshot 2026 02 06 At 19 07 26](/images/Screenshot2026-02-06at19.07.26.png)

## Key metrics

![Screenshot 2026 02 06 At 19 09 35](/images/Screenshot2026-02-06at19.09.35.png)

**Active Balance:** Total amount currently invested across all Byzantine products

**APY (Annual Percentage Yield):** Current annualized return rate

**Returns Display:**

- Yesterday's earnings
- Last 30 days earnings
- Year-to-date earnings

## Byzantine Prime products

![Screenshot 2026 02 06 At 19 09 50](/images/Screenshot2026-02-06at19.09.50.png)

- **Prime USD** - US Dollar yield product (uninsured)
- **Prime EUR** - Euro yield product (uninsured)
- **Prime EUR ⚡ Insured** - Euro yield product with insurance coverage
- **Prime USD ⚡ Insured** - US Dollar yield product with insurance coverage

## Vault View

![Screenshot 2026 02 06 At 19 09 13](/images/Screenshot2026-02-06at19.09.13.png)

Click on any product to see:

- Transaction history (deposits/withdrawals)
- Detailed performance metrics
- Current APY for that specific product
