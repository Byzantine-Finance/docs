## Step 1: Initiate Withdrawal

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click **"Withdraw"** on the product you want to withdraw from
3. Enter the amount
4. Authenticate with your passkey

## Step 2: Bank account verification

**First withdrawal:** You'll need to specify your destination bank account. This account is:

- Locked after first use (cannot be changed without admin approval)
- Must match the account holder name on your Byzantine account
- Verified for security

**Funds arrive in your bank account within 10 minutes for sums until 150k, and can take up to 24h above that.**
