## Step 1: Initiate Withdrawal

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click **"Withdraw"** on the product you want to withdraw from
3. Enter the amount
4. Authenticate with your passkey

## Step 2: Bank account verification

**First withdrawal:** You'll need to specify your destination bank account. This account:

- Can only be added or removed by admin users
- Must match the account holder name on your Byzantine account
- Is verified for security

<Info>
Funds arrive in your bank account within 10 minutes for sums up to €100,000, and can take up to 24h above that.
</Info>
