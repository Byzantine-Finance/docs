This section explains how to manage existing Byzantine accounts once KYC/KYB is complete.

Typical operations include:

- **Managing bank accounts**: adding payout / off-ramp bank accounts for an existing `account_id`.
- **Managing access**: inviting new users to an account and updating their roles.
- **Reviewing invitations**: listing pending invitations associated with an account or email.

Use this page together with the **Account data** and **Account management** endpoints in the OpenAPI-powered **API endpoints** section.
