## Adding team members

Multiple users can be invited to the same account.

**There are two user types:**

| **User type** | **Can view** | **Can deposit** | **Can withdraw** | **Can change settings** |
| :------------ | :----------- | :-------------- | :--------------- | :---------------------- |
| **Admin**     | ✅            | ✅               | ✅                | ✅                       |
| **View-only** | ✅            | ❌               | ❌                | ❌                       |

## Recommended Setup for Family Offices

**Admin users:**

- Client/beneficial owner
- Authorized signatories

**View-only users:**

- Wealth managers
- Accountants/bookkeepers
- Advisory team members

**Security:** Only users with banking access can complete deposits (via QR code or by accessing the source bank account).
