## Can multiple people access the same Byzantine account?

Yes. You can add unlimited users with either Admin or View-only permissions. The multi-user feature will be available next week.

## Can we use multiple email addresses for notifications?

Yes. Each user you invite will receive their own notifications. You can also configure notification preferences.

## Who should create the account - the wealth manager or the client?

Either can create the account. The important requirement is that the Ultimate Beneficial Owner(s) and at least one authorized representative complete identity verification. The account creator can be anyone on the team.
